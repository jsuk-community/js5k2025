On the day:

When should I arrive?
????????Arrival is from 6:00pm - you will be allocated a registration time, please check your emails! IF you have not received an email with your registration time, please arrive to check-in at the Bowling Green for 6pm! For more information on the schedule, take a look at our events page here.
What happens if I arrive late?
The course will close at 8pm. We recommend arriving at least 45 minutes early.
Where does the race start and finish?
The race will start near Bowling Green and end at [location]. Save this map for reference, and there will be signs and marshals to guide you on the spot too - don't worry!
What if I'm unable to attend the race?
We're sorrr you couldn't join us, but unfortunately all race entries are non-refundable.
Will the race go ahead if it's very hot or raining?
Yes! Come rain or shine, the event will be going ahead. If an extreme weather event occurs on race day, we will alert participants of plans as soon as possible.
Are dogs allowed in the event site?
Due to the scale of the event - unfortunately not!

Race & Bibs

Do I have to wear a bib?
Yes, that's the only way we can track your time!
How will I receive my bib?
You will receive the bib a couple weeks in advance via post.
What do I do if I haven't received my bib via post?
DO NOT WORRY! You can pick up a bib at our check-in desk when you arrive. Please have time confirmation email ready and we'll sort it out.

Logistics

Will you have a Bag Drop?
There will be a small bag drop at Bowling Green but we expect this to be very busy so please avoid bringing bags if possible or bring a spectator to watch bags!
Is water provided?
There are water fountains in the park, and bottled water will be available. But we strongly advise you to bring your water as well!
Are food and drinks available?
Yes, we'll have amazing food & beverage vendors around onsite to fuel everyone (whether you run or not)!

Race Results:

When will I receive my event results?
As soon as you cross the line you'll receive it via text. The timing team will be at the event if you have any questions.
